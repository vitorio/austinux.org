Austin UX

User experience, interaction design and other design-related activities

New to the Austin design community? Watch this video and read these slides from our June 2015 talk.

Skip down to the calendar of events.

This site curates UX-related design events from IxDA Austin, Austin UXPA, AIGA Austin, STC Austin, the Austin Design and Visual Arts Network, Refresh Austin, the School of Information, the Department of Art and Art History, the Austin Content Meetup, Agile Austin UX SIG, the ATXDribbble Meetup, the Austin Mobile App Design and Art Group, Lean User Experience Austin, CreativeMornings/Austin, the Visual Arts Center, the UX Book Club: Austin, Enterprise UX Austin, Austin Initiative for Graphic Awesomeness, Hot Topics in UX: Finding Expert Answers, Ladies That UX, Austin, General Assembly, Austin UX Researchers, Wrkshop and Fresh to Design in ATX. Did I miss something?

  • 1st Tuesday: Austin UXPA
  • 1st Wednesday, every other month: IxDA Austin UX lunch
  • 2nd Tuesday: Refresh Austin, 7pm at Buffalo Billiards
  • 3rd Thursday: Fresh to Design in ATX happy hour
  • Everyone else meets irregularly

Want weekly reminder emails about upcoming events? You should subscribe to the mailing list.

If you want these events to show up in your Google or Outlook or iOS calendars, or your newsreader, visit http://plancast.com/austinux. There’s a bunch of icons in the upper right: the weird shopping-bag-eye is calendar export, and the angled wifi is RSS export.

You might also be interested in the Austin UX LinkedIn group run by Craig Moser, the #design-talk channel in the Refresh Austin Slack, the #local_austin channel in the UX Community Slack, the Austin UX/UI Slack, or the Design Community Twitter Hours (#dcth) Slack (message Cecy Correa on Twitter for access).

Cory Lebson wrote a nice thing about Austin UX for UX Magazine, The Hidden Value of UX Connectors and the Future of Our Community.
